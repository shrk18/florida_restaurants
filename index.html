Recommended Restaurants in Florida

  • Pacific Rim - Located in Merritt Island, this restaurant offers a unique blend of Asian and American cuisine.
  • Pho Quyen - A popular spot in Pinellas Park known for its authentic Vietnamese dishes.
  • Keys Fisheries - Situated in Marathon Key, this restaurant is famous for its fresh seafood and casual atmosphere.
  • The Beach Bistro - Located on Anna Maria Island, this bistro is known for its stunning views and gourmet dining experience.
  • Bern's Steak House - A legendary restaurant in Tampa, renowned for its exceptional steaks and extensive wine list.

For more options, you can also explore the MICHELIN Guide for Florida, which lists starred restaurants and Bib Gourmand selections.

Enjoy your trip and happy dining!
